Book Pickup & Delivery Policy

Last Updated: November 24, 2025

Readar is a local community-based book sharing platform. Books are exchanged through in-person pickups arranged between buyers and sellers. This policy outlines how book handovers work.

Platform Model

Readar facilitates peer-to-peer book exchanges. We do not warehouse, ship, or deliver books. All physical exchanges are coordinated directly between users.

Pickup Process

  1. Reservation: Buyer reserves a book and pays the full price through PhonePe
  2. Seller Confirmation: Seller confirms book availability and pickup readiness
  3. Coordination: Buyer and seller coordinate pickup location and time through the platform
  4. Exchange: Buyer picks up the book at the agreed location
  5. Confirmation: Seller marks the book as collected in the platform

Pickup Timeline

  • Sellers must make books available within 7 days of reservation confirmation
  • Buyers must pick up books within 7 days of seller's availability confirmation
  • If pickup does not occur within this timeframe, either party may request cancellation

Pickup Locations

Sellers may choose pickup locations such as:

  • Public places (coffee shops, libraries, bookstores)
  • Residential areas (with mutual agreement)
  • Community centers or local landmarks

For safety, we recommend meeting in public, well-lit locations.

Book Rentals

For rented books:

  • Pickup process is the same as purchases
  • Return must be coordinated within the rental period
  • Late returns may incur additional fees
  • Books must be returned in the same condition

Safety Guidelines

  • Always meet in public, populated areas
  • Inform someone about your meeting location and time
  • Verify the book condition before completing the exchange
  • Use the platform's messaging system to coordinate details
  • Never share personal financial information outside the platform

Delivery Options (Future)

While Readar currently supports only local pickups, we are exploring:

  • Optional courier delivery for users willing to pay shipping costs
  • Partnerships with local delivery services
  • Community drop-off points

These features will be announced when available.

Issues with Pickup

If you encounter problems:

  • Seller No-Show: Report through the platform; may be eligible for refund
  • Book Condition Mismatch: You may reject the book and request refund with evidence
  • Safety Concerns: Report inappropriate behavior immediately
  • Communication Issues: Contact support for assistance

Seller Responsibilities

  • Respond to pickup coordination messages within 48 hours
  • Provide accurate book descriptions and conditions
  • Make books available as promised
  • Be punctual for scheduled pickup meetings
  • Mark transactions as complete after handover

Buyer Responsibilities

  • Communicate clearly about pickup preferences
  • Be punctual for scheduled pickups
  • Verify book condition before leaving the meeting
  • Complete the transaction confirmation on the platform
