Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: November 24, 2025

This policy outlines the conditions under which Readar provides refunds and cancellations for book reservations and transactions processed through PhonePe Business Payments Gateway.

Cancellation Policy

Before Book Handover:

  • Users may cancel a reservation before the seller marks the book as ready for pickup
  • Cancellation requests must be submitted through the platform or by contacting support
  • Once the seller confirms availability for pickup, the transaction is considered final

After Reservation Confirmation:

  • If the seller fails to provide the book within 7 days of reservation, buyers may request cancellation
  • If the book condition significantly differs from the description, buyers may reject and request cancellation at pickup

Refund Eligibility

Refunds are provided only under the following conditions:

  • Duplicate Payment: If you were charged multiple times for the same transaction
  • Seller No-Show: If the seller fails to provide the book within 7 days of confirmation
  • Misrepresented Book: If the book condition is significantly worse than described and verified by platform review
  • Payment Error: If payment was processed but the reservation was not created due to a technical error
  • Seller Cancellation: If the seller cancels after receiving payment

Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will NOT be provided for:

  • Change of mind after the seller confirms book availability
  • Buyer fails to pick up the book within the agreed timeframe
  • Minor differences in book condition within reasonable expectations
  • Personal preferences about the book content
  • Completed rental transactions (rental fees are non-refundable once the book is borrowed)

Refund Process

  1. Request Submission: Email eazt7ujher@gmail.com with your reservation ID, transaction details, and reason for refund
  2. Review: We will review your request within 2-3 business days
  3. Approval: If approved, refunds will be processed through PhonePe to your original payment method
  4. Processing Time: Refunds typically appear in your account within 5-7 business days after approval

Rental Returns

For book rentals:

  • Books must be returned by the due date in the same condition
  • Late returns may incur additional fees as specified in the rental agreement
  • Damaged or lost books may require payment of the book's replacement value
  • No refunds on rental fees once the borrowing period begins

Dispute Resolution

If you disagree with a refund decision:

  • You may submit additional evidence within 7 days of the decision
  • We will conduct a final review and provide a binding decision
  • All decisions are made at our sole discretion based on available evidence

Payment Gateway Refunds

All refunds are processed through PhonePe Business Payments Gateway. Refund timelines depend on PhonePe's processing schedule and your bank's policies. Readar is not responsible for delays in refund processing by third-party payment processors or financial institutions.
